**Initial Release 6**



**Symbolic Value : Limits**



**MAX\_ACTION\_DEPTH** **-** Maximum
depth of sub actions


**----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------**



**#include <actods.h>**



**Description :**



This is the
maximum depth of sub actions. A back slash "\" separates each level
in the action's title. Here's an example action title:
"one\two\three\four\five\six\seven\eight"
