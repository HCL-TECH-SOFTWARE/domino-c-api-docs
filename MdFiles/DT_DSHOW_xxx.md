**DT\_DSHOW\_xxx** **-** CDEXT2FIELD
- DTDShow


**----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------**



**#include <misc.h>**


 **Symbolic Values :**      DT\_DSHOW\_ALL     -  Show all the date values: day of the
week, day, month, and year.  

  

      DT\_DSHOW\_YM      -  Show only year and month.  

  

      DT\_DSHOW\_WMD   -  Show day of the week, month, and day.  

  

      DT\_DSHOW\_W        -  Show day of the week.  

  

      DT\_DSHOW\_M        -  Show month.  

  

      DT\_DSHOW\_MD      -  Show month and day.  

  

      DT\_DSHOW\_MDY    -  Shows month, day and year.  

  

      DT\_DSHOW\_D         -  Shows day.  

  

      DT\_DSHOW\_Y         -  Shows year.  

  




**Description :**



The Show
format in the Display Date.
