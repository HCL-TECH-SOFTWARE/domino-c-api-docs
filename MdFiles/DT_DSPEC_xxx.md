**DT\_DSPEC\_xxx** **-** CDEXT2FIELD
- DTDSpecial


**----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------**



**#include <misc.h>**


 **Symbolic Values :**      DT\_DSPEC\_NONE   -  No special handling  

  

      DT\_DSPEC\_TODAY             -  Use 'Today', 'Yesterday', 'Tomorrow', when
possible  

  

      DT\_DSPEC\_Y4        -  Always display year on OUTPUT as 4 digit year  

  

      DT\_DSPEC\_21Y4     -  Show 4 digit year for 21st century  

  

      DT\_DSPEC\_CURYR             -  Display year when not the current year  

  




**Description :**



The
"Special" format for the "Display Date".
