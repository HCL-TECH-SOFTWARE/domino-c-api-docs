**AREA\_SHAPE\_xxx** **-** Defines
Shape in a CDAREAELEMENT.


**----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------**



**#include <editods.h>**


 **Symbolic Values :**      AREA\_SHAPE\_RECT           -  Graphic area is a rectangle.  

  

      AREA\_SHAPE\_CIRCLE        -  Graphic area is a circle.  

  

      AREA\_SHAPE\_POLYGON    -  Graphic area is polygon.  

  

      AREA\_SHAPE\_DEFAULT     -  Graphic area is a hotspot.  

  




**Description :**



Constants
which define the shape within a CDAREAELEMENT record.
