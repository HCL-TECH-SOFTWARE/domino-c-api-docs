**Symbolic Value : Constants**



**ONEINCH** **-** Number of
TWIPS in an inch


**----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------**



**#include <editdflt.h>**



**Description :**



The value of
ONEINCH is the number of TWIPS in an inch.  All units in the fields of the
CDPABDEFINITION structure are in TWIPS (1/20 of a point).  There are 72 points
to an inch and 20 TWIPS to a point, and therefore there are 72 \* 20 TWIPS to an
inch.
