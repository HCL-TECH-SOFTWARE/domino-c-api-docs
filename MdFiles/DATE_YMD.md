**Symbolic Value : Time**



**DATE\_YMD** **-** INTLFORMAT -
Flags (Year Month Day date format).


**----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------**



**#include <intl.h>**



**Description :**



The
international environment parameter definitions are flags stored in the Flags
member of an INTLFORMAT structure. The flag settings can be determined by
bitwise ANDing the WORD with each define in a conditional expression.
