**Symbolic Value : Views**



**VIEW\_STYLE\_xxx** **-** VIEW\_FORMAT\_HEADER
- ViewStyle


**----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------**



**#include <viewfmt.h>**


 **Symbolic Values :**      VIEW\_STYLE\_TABLE           -  Table style view.  

  

      VIEW\_STYLE\_DAY   -  Calendar view, day style.  

  

      VIEW\_STYLE\_WEEK           -  Calendar view, week style.  

  

      VIEW\_STYLE\_MONTH         -  Calendar view, month style.  

  




**Description :**



These
symbols are the possible values for the ViewStyle member of the
VIEW\_FORMAT\_HEADER, specifying the style of a view.
