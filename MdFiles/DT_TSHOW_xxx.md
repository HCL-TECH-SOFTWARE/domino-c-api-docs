**DT\_TSHOW\_xxx** **-** CDEXT2FIELD
- DTTShow


**----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------**



**#include <misc.h>**


 **Symbolic Values :**      DT\_TSHOW\_H         -  Displays hours only.  

  

      DT\_TSHOW\_HM      -  Display hours and minutes.  

  

      DT\_TSHOW\_HMS    -  Display hours, minutes and seconds.  

  

      DT\_TSHOW\_ALL     -  Display the complete time.  

  




**Description :**



The
"Show" format of the "Display Time".
